Welcome to My Lab Website

I am an Assistant Professor and Principal Investigator at Virginia Tech, dedicated to advancing scientific research and mentoring the next generation of scholars. My lab focuses on innovative solutions in aquaculture, seafood science, and sustainable resource management.

This website serves as a central hub for research information, lab resources, and collaborative opportunities. For more detailed information about my background and research, please visit my Virginia Tech faculty profile.

About My Research

My research group is committed to understanding and solving critical challenges in aquaculture and seafood science. We employ integrative approaches combining experimental research, data analysis, and stakeholder engagement to develop sustainable practices that benefit both the industry and the environment.

Our work addresses topics including but not limited to: aquaculture management, seafood quality and safety, sustainable production systems, and resource conservation.
